Teaching my Boyfriend Chess

In an effort to get my boyfriend to pay attention to anything but football, I thought I'd try to teach him chess. I have a lot of experience with the game, but my boyfriend has none. I explained the names of the chess pieces and where they can move. I told him, like all games, the best way to learn is to play. We proceeded to play. It was like teaching a monkey how to play chess. He did not understand at all. He kept moving pieces one spot on the board and trying to hop pieces to capture my king. He kept trying to make the game end faster so that he could go back to watching football. I did not let him off so easily. I told him to play 3 proper games of chess and then he could go back to football. It was really funny to watch him and I learned a valuable lesson.
