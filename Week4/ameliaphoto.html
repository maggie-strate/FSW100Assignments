Description of Amelia Photography

Amelia Photography is a photography company owned and operated by Maggie Strate, Photographer. Amelia Photography serves St. Cloud, MN and surrounding areas as well as the general public with freelance services. Amelia Photography specializes in portrait photography, miscellaneous freelance, and does special events and nature photography for fun. Maggie has been a hobby photographer for most of her life, but founded Amelia Photography in April 2019. Her photography is unique in that she has a sense of adventure, she brings the experience to the client, and her eye for detail is unmatched. She has been certified in the photography masterclass and is proficient in many editing platforms as well. During a photo session with Maggie, you can expect professionalism, fun, creativity and most importantly a beautiful end product.
